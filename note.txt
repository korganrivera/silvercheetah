To pipe a bunch of csv files to silvercheetah one at a time, you can do this:
    ls csv/* | xargs -n1 silvercheetah

This will create tss.log with empty elements.

Next, prep tss.log to be processed by freshcheetah.

    sort -nu tss.log > tss.temp
    cat tss.temp > tss.log
    rm tss.temp"

And then

    freshcheetah tss.log
